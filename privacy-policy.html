Privacy Policy

ZaKalculator  ·  Last updated: February 28, 2026

Overview

ZaKalculator is a zakah calculation tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

All information you enter — asset holdings, prices, payment records, and profiles — is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, transmit, or disclose any user data to any third parties. There are no servers, databases, or cloud services involved.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

Data Export / Backup

The app includes an optional backup and restore feature that creates a JSON file saved to your device. This file is controlled entirely by you and is not shared with us.

Permissions

The app requests access to device storage only for the optional backup and restore feature. No other device permissions are used.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please open an issue on GitHub.
